Tripper Truck

A Tripper Truck (also known as a Dump Truck) is designed for hauling and unloading bulk materials such as sand, gravel, and construction debris. Equipped with a hydraulic lifting mechanism, the truckâ€™s bed tilts to quickly offload materials, making it essential for construction, mining, and heavy-duty transport.

  • High-load capacity for bulk materials
  • Durable hydraulic tipping system
  • Suitable for rugged terrains and heavy-duty applications

Containers

Container trucks are built to transport standardized shipping containers, making them the backbone of global logistics and freight movement. They are available in 20ft, 40ft, and 45ft configurations, catering to different shipping requirements.

  • Compatible with international container sizes
  • Secure locking system for stable transport
  • Suitable for long-distance and cross-border shipping

Open Trucks

Open Trucks are versatile, flatbed-style trucks that are ideal for carrying oversized or irregularly shaped cargo. Without side enclosures, they allow for easy loading and unloading of heavy machinery, construction materials, and industrial goods.

  • Unrestricted loading from all sides
  • Ideal for large or unconventional cargo
  • Commonly used in construction, agriculture, and industrial transport

Bulk Cement Truck Trailers

Bulk Cement Truck Trailers are specially designed for transporting dry bulk materials like cement, fly ash, and lime powder. These trailers feature a sealed tanker body with air compressor systems to ensure efficient, dust-free unloading.

  • Large-capacity sealed tank for bulk dry materials
  • Pneumatic unloading system for easy discharge
  • Ideal for cement manufacturers and construction companies

Flatbed Trailers

Flatbed Trailers provide an open, level surface to carry heavy and oversized loads such as steel beams, machinery, and construction equipment. With no side walls or roof, they allow for easy crane or forklift loading.

  • Ideal for large and heavy cargo
  • Easy loading and unloading from any direction
  • Common in logistics, construction, and manufacturing industries
